The Challenge

Education Inequality in Tunisia

Many rural areas in Tunisia face significant educational challenges:

  • Distance & Accessibility: Limited access to schools in remote regions
  • Infrastructure Deficiency: Poor urban planning and lack of transport
  • Digital Divide: Unequal access to digital learning resources
  • Regional Disparity: Uneven development across different areas

Root Causes:

Uneven regional development, poor urban planning, low population density in rural zones, and lack of school transport infrastructure.
